Healthy Chicken Pad Thai

Ingredients for 4-6 servings

Main:

  • 1 raw chicken breast
  • 200g flat brown rice noodles
  • 2 tbsp finely chopped garlic
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 shredded carrot
  • 1 cup mushrooms (optional)
  • 1.5 cup green onions (chopped)
  • 2 cup bean sprouts
  • 1.5 cup Firm tofu
  • fresh parsley

Sauce:

  • 1/8 cup rice vinegar
  • 1/8 cup fish sauce
  • 1/8 cup Coconut Aminos (low sodium soy sauce)
  • 1/8 cup Red chili pepper sauce
  • 3 tbsp coconut sugar
  • juice from 1 lime
  • Juice from 1/2 mandarin orange
  • 3 tsp tomato paste

Instructions

  1. Soak brown rice noodles in warm water. Drain and set aside when softened.
  2. Chop up veggies – bell pepper, carrot, mushrooms, green onions, parsley.
  3. Slice chicken breast into small pieces. Marinate with 1 tbsp corn starch, 1tsp coconut amino, garlic powder, white pepper.
  4. In a bowl, mix rice vinegar, fish sauce, coconut sugar, red chili pepper sauce, tomato paste, fresh orange juice & fresh lime juice.
  5. Warm a pan with coconut oil, then add garlic. Toss in chicken breast pieces and cook.
  6. Add in fresh veggies with a bit of sauce.
  7. Finally, incorporate noodles and mix in remaining sauce.

Broccoli Burgers



Ingredients for 10-12 burgers

For burgers:

  • 2 heads of broccoli, washed and cut
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 medium potato, diced
  • 1-2 carrots, peeled
  • 1-2 sweet peppers
  • 1 onion, peeled and sliced
  • 1 sweet potato, wrapped in foil

Seasoning:

  • avocado oil
  • Himalayan salt
  • garlic powder
  • pepper
  • 2 tsp corn starch

Instructions

  1. Place cut up onion, carrots, peppers into a large glass mixing bowl.
  2. Season with avocado oil, salt, pepper, garlic powder and toss.
  3. Place vegetables and sweet potato onto a baking pan then roast in the oven at 375 degrees for 25 minutes or until soft.
  4. Put diced potato into the glass bowl and microwave for 3 minutes or until soft enough to mash.
  5. Place cut up broccoli into a food processor and process until it is finely chopped. Add broccoli to the potato in the bowl.
  6. Add roasted vegetables to the food processor and pulse until finely chopped. Add to the mixing bowl.
  7. In bowl, mix all the ingredients together with eggs, corn starch, and a sprinkle of salt, pepper, and garlic powder until fully incorporated. The mix should be solid enough to form patties.
  8. Form mix into balls, flatten them into patties, and then pan fry them on medium heat with a bit of avocado oil.

Raw Vegan Avocado Lemon Tarts



I originally wanted to make key lime pie but had lemons, so therefore they became lemon tarts. You can sub in any nut you want too.


Ingredients

Crust:

  • 1 cup dried unsweetened shredded coconut
  • 1 cup dry almonds (not soaked)
  • 3/4 to 1 cup organic raisins (or 4-6 medjool dates)
  • pinch of himalayan salt

Filling:

  • 2 small avocados
  • 1 cup soaked almonds(or cashews, any nut you like)
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp orange zest
  • 1/3 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
  • 2 tsp pure vanilla extract or 1/2 vanilla bean
  • 1/4 cup of coconut oil (melted)

Instructions

For the crust:

  1. Place coconut flakes, almonds, raisins and slat into food processor and blend until mixture starts to come together when squeezed.
  2. Place muffin cups into muffin try and press the mixture into the bottom and sides of the try, to form a cup. OR if you’re doing a cake, use a 6” springform pan and press into the bottom.
  3. Place into fridge or freezer to chill while you prepare the filling.

For the filling:

  1. Combine avocado, almonds, lemon, zest, maple syrup, salt, coconut oil, and vanilla into food processor or blender. Process until smooth.
  2. Put filling into the the crusts and place in fridge or freezer for 2-3 hours to chill and firm up.
  3. Enjoy!!
